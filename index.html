About Me

Hello! I’m Santhosh Reddy Methukupally, a tech enthusiast with a knack for analytical thinking and a love for constant learning. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, I cherish spending time with my family and friends, finding joy in the simple things that bring us closer.

When I’m not diving into the world of technology, you’ll likely find me playing cricket with my friends, cheering for my favorite team, Royal Challengers Bangalore, or indulging in a good movie. My love for cricket stems from the sheer excitement the game brings, and it’s a passion I carry off the field as well.

I’m also a huge fan of Biryani—the quintessential Hyderabadi delicacy—and while I enjoy cooking, I’ll admit it’s a work in progress! Beyond the fun, I believe in the power of spirituality and deeply value equality in all walks of life.

With a Master’s degree in Computer Science, I’m constantly driven by the ever-evolving world of technology. I thrive on staying updated and applying my analytical mindset to solve complex problems. If I were to describe myself with a spiritual animal, it would undoubtedly be a Panda—calm, thoughtful, and a fan of life’s simple joys.
